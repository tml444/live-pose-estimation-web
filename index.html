How to use:
  • Allow camera access when prompted
  • Click "Start Recording" to begin capturing pose data and video simultaneously
  • Click "Stop Recording" when finished
  • Export pose data as JSON (structured) or CSV (spreadsheet-compatible)
  • Export video as WebM file for synchronization with pose data
  • Keep the tab active and screen on during recording

Data synchronization: Pose data timestamps match video timeline. Use the relativeTime field in JSON/CSV to sync with video playback.

Data format: Each frame contains 33 landmarks with x, y, z coordinates and visibility scores, plus timestamp.
